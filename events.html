States' Debate - July 15th

The States are due to debate the Proposals for a New Discrimination Ordinance on the 15th July 2020. We need as many people as possible to come along at 8.30am on July 15 to the steps of the Royal Court to show our support for the proposal to be approved, and demonstrate to the deputies how important it is to protect our people from discrimination with a legislation, and to educate the community. Click here to download a printable sign to stick on cardboard and bring to the steps. See you there!
