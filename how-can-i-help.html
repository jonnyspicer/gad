How Can I Help?

Show your support in person

Come to the steps of the Royal Court in support of approving of the proposals at the States’ debate which is scheduled to take place on July 15. Be there at 8.30am. Contact us for more information.

Email your deputies

Email deputies to show your support of approving the Proposals for a New Discrimination Ordinance. If you are not sure what to say, copy this template (or use the information in it to write your own message) and use this form which sends an email to all deputies. Alternatively, you can email specific deputies – find their contact details here.

Sign petitions

Sign this petition to push for the approval of the proposal.

Sign this petition to add education on diversity and racism to all school curriculums in the UK (this should hopefully then be extended to Guernsey).

Sign this petition to push for Britain’s colonial past to be a compulsory part of the curriculum (this should hopefully then be extended to Guernsey).
