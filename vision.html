Our Vision

We are striving to raise awareness of the importance of protecting minority groups from discrimination in the Guernsey community.

Our current focus is pushing for the Proposals for a New Discrimination Ordinance to be approved at the Statesâ€™ debate on the 15th July.

We are pressing for anti-racism education in Guernsey, as well as increased awareness of unconscious bias and discrimination in the workplace, in schools and across the community.

Who We Are

We are a campaign group consisting of seven passionate young people from Guernsey, aspiring to change attitudes in the community regarding racism and discrimination. We are all either studying or have studied off-island at university, and were shocked to learn that Guernsey is the only jurisdiction in Europe (other than Sark and Alderney) not to have a law prohibiting discrimination on the basis of race, disability, sexual orientation, religion and age, in employment, education or provision of goods and services etc.
